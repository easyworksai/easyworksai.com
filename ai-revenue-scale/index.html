What it does

Five things, running 24/7.

01

Auto budget reallocation

Every 4 hours, the AI moves money from the ads losing you money to the ads making you money. Most agencies check monthly — if at all.

02

Creative rotation + A/B

Multiple versions of each ad, auto-rotated. The duds get cut, the winners get more budget. Fresh ads made weekly if you've added the Content Engine.

03

Know exactly what worked

We track the full path — ad click to form to call to booked job. No more guessing which ad actually brought the customer in.

04

Audience tuning

Lookalikes refresh weekly off your closed-won list. AI flags the segments that close fastest and biases spend toward them.

05

Plain-English reports

You don't read ad dashboards. You get a weekly text: "Spent $X. Got Y leads. Z closed. Here's what we changed."
